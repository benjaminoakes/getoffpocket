Have a Kobo?

Or another e-Reader?

Recommended Solution: Wallabag + KOReader

A significant number of Kobo users relied on Pocket's integration to read articles on their devices.

With its discontinuation, KOReader emerges as the most viable alternative we know of. It is a versatile reader that you install on your device. It can be configured to sync with services like Wallabag, allowing you to continue your reading workflow.

KOReader is supported on a wide array of platforms:

  • Android
  • ChromeOS
  • Kindle
  • Kobo
  • PocketBook
  • reMarkable
  • Desktop Linux
  • macOS
  • Windows (with WSL)

Wallabag

A mature, feature-rich read-it-later application. Wallabag has been actively developed since 2013 and offers comprehensive article management, tagging, annotations, and mobile apps. The most established open-source option with the largest community.

KOReader

An powerhouse, versatile e-reading application. KOReader is a long-standing project that provides extensive customization, broad format support, and powerful in-book navigation tools. The premier open-source choice for dedicated e-ink devices, supported by a vibrant community.
